A bit about my work

I'm a Data & Business Intelligence Analyst based in Bhopal, India. My work sits at the intersection of data engineering, analytics, and storytelling — I take data from wherever it lives, clean it up, model it, and turn it into something a decision-maker can actually act on.

Data analytics, at its core, is about asking the right questions. I work across the full pipeline — writing SQL queries that surface patterns, building Python-based data models, and packaging everything into Power BI dashboards that non-technical stakeholders can explore themselves. The goal is always the same: replace gut-feel with evidence.
